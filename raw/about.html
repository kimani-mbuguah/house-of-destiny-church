THE CHURCH HISTORY

The House of Destiny Ministries was Launched in Kerugoya town in December 2013. It began with just a handful of committed members who prayerfully dedicated themselves to the service of God. The Church was in a small hall at the town centre but within one year the hall became too small for the growth that had been experienced and therefore the church relocated in February 2014 to their present location.

THE FOCUS OF THE CHURCH

The Church focuses it's effort mostly to the youth in the county. Every school term, the Church engages in high schools and colleges missions in the county. In the year 2015, over 1800 decisions for Christ were made in High schools only. Year 2017, over 7000 Bibles were given to high school students in Kirinyaga, County.

OUR PROGRAMS

The Church program is based on outreach program in our Community. Some of the programs include:

  • Hospital missions, three times a week.
  • Kids outreach program, once a week.
  • Youth Connect, a meeting for youths every week.
  • Vacational Bible school for Kids(VBS) every School holiday.
  • Bible study every week.
  • Prison ministry.

OUR CORE VALUES

  • The Holy Bible is our final authority
  • We value people; we are a people centered church
  • We cherish teamwork
  • We treasure excellence
  • We are committed to transparency
